You must or you don't have choose! Absolutely the best food for all CS students!

Instant noodles, the brilliant brainchild hailing from Asia's street food legends, and gloriously wiggly, rubbery strands paired with a neon seasoning packet that transforms any mundane bowl into a chaotic festival

Beloved by broke students and sleep-deprives night owls alike, these humble noodles deliver a three-minute gourmet miracle, turning boiling water into a sizzling.

I'm too lazy for a Michelin star, but give me flavor!



Ingredients (AKA the key for surviving life)

  • 1 pack of instant noodles
  • Hot water (boiling, because lukewarm sadness won't do)
  • The mystery seasoning packet
  • An egg (a optional, bue make you feel like a MasterChef)
  • Some leftover veggies (a desperate attempt at being healthy)
  • Cheese (why not? life is already chaotic)

Steps

  1. Boil some water (the hardest part)
  2. Throw the instant noodles into the bubbling chaos
  3. Wait 3 mins (or 2, or 4, even 10. it doesn't matter how long as long as you willing to eat it.)
  4. Rip open the seasoning packet and sprinkle that MSG magic
  5. Throw in an egg, veggies, or whether makes you feel like a genius
  6. Serve in a bowl or other pot. No judgement
  7. Devour it with VS code or other development environment, whatever you like.
